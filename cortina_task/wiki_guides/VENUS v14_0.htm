Server Connect

Instructions

Enter a Server Name and a Service Port and click SCAN! to connect to a server and scan for available asics/fpgas to view.

Protocol: "rpc" to connect to driver servers, "spy" to connect to RTL models. "xrpc" to connect to the new "lab4" environment. You may need to specify a custom LD_LIBRARY_PATH to connect to some (rpc) driver servers.

Database: Specify a reg database, if other than "reg.db".

Or: Click on SHOW ALL to get a list of known asic/fpga register viewers that you can choose from to browse.
